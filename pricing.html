What Sets Us Apart

Why Choose PBJ?

Two things you'll never find here: cookie-cutter packages or surprise invoices.

Built Around Your Business

At PB&J Strategic Accounting, we understand that no two businesses are exactly alike. A growing construction company in Clay County doesn't have the same financial challenges as a Jacksonville startup or a local family-owned shop. That's why we design customized accounting packages to fit your specific needs, budget, and goals.

Maybe you only need basic monthly bookkeeping to keep your QuickBooks organized. Or perhaps your business is scaling quickly and requires fractional CFO support for cash flow forecasting, budgeting, and financial strategy. We can adjust your package to include exactly what you need — and nothing you don't.

Our flexible approach means you'll never feel boxed into a "one-size-fits-all" service. Instead, you'll get a tailored solution that grows alongside your business, providing the right level of support at the right time.

Straightforward Pricing, No Surprises

Running a business is challenging enough without the added stress of keeping your books in order or worrying if your financials are accurate. At PB&J Strategic Accounting, we believe bookkeeping shouldn't feel overwhelming, it should give you confidence and clarity. That's why we offer transparent pricing and tailored solutions for businesses in Clay County and Jacksonville who need dependable support.

Whether you're struggling with QuickBooks cleanup, want reliable monthly bookkeeping, or need the strategic guidance of a fractional CFO, our team is here to help. We don't just crunch numbers, we partner with you to understand your goals, identify opportunities, and give you the financial insight you need to make smart business decisions.

Our pricing is straightforward, with no hidden fees or surprise charges. You'll know exactly what services you're receiving and what value you're getting. From startups to established businesses, we work with clients at every stage to provide bookkeeping and CFO services that scale as you grow.

No contracts. Transparent billing, always.
