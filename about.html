Meet Your Accountant

Hi, I'm Brittany Ferguson

Founder & Lead Accountant at PB&J Strategic Accounting

After 13 years working in accounting, I decided to venture out and take the knowledge I had gained over the years to truly help people and companies understand their sticky financial situations.

I specialize in helping Clay County and surrounding area small business owners - contractors, service providers, startups, and family businesses - get their books in order and their finances working for them.

I love taking those messy sandwiches and turning them into an enjoyable feast everyone has come to love. The name PBJ comes from my three wonderful children - and yes, I am as detail-oriented as the name implies.
