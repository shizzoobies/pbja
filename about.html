Meet Your Accountant

Hi, I'm Brittany Ferguson

Founder & Lead Accountant at PBJ Strategic Accounting

After 13 years working in accounting, I decided to venture out and take the knowledge I had gained over the years to truly help people and companies understand their sticky financial situations.

I specialize in helping Clay County and Jacksonville small business owners — contractors, service providers, startups, and family businesses — get their books in order and their finances working for them.
